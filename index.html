    • My Story

    Chop chop, slice slice, smash smash. A few years ago, those were the sounds that I was constantly hearing. Back then, those sounds were what I expected to be hearing every working day for the rest of my life. Today, I could not work in a more different setting, behind a computer screen, typing away with my fingertips.

    As a former chef, I loved cooking dishes. I loved, and still do love, the creativity and detail that goes behind making technical, beautiful and of course tasty dishes. Food is such an integral part of our lives, and can have such a positive and touching effect on people (just talk to my wife when she's hangry). But I wanted more. Something different, something that I felt could impact society and those around me just a little more.

    Then it happened. That magical moment. My genie came in the form of Professor Maloney, who taught the first programming class I ever took at my local community college. After that first class, it was like she had sprinkled some pixie dust on me; I knew I had found a new passion and future career. Programming was something that gave me the opportunity to continue to feed my love of problem solving and creativity, but in a position that had the potential to transform the entire structure of our society. This is what drives me now, the potential to affect and change the lives of people around me, and around the entire world.

    Work

    LairBnB

    LairBnB is a full stack clone of AirBnB that connects villains who want to rent out their evil lairs to everyday citizens seeking something less mundane for their vacation homes.

    Technologies used: JavaScript, React, Node.js, MongoDB, Express, Heroku, BCrypt, AWS S3, SCSS, CSS, Heroku

    Github Live Site

    Splash page allows to search for lairs without logging in.

    Scrolling in and out of the Google Map API dynamically adjusts the lairs you can see on the left depending on which pins are shown.

    Search lairs by location, categories (castles, spaceships, etc..). If you're feeling fancy, explore the evil luxury lairs.

    Book a lair using the React-Dates calendar as well as view its description, amenities, and reviews on this beautiful lair show page.

    View all of the trips that you have planned! Cancelling the trip will remove it without refreshing the page.

    Disinterest

    Disinterest is a full stack clone of Pinterest where users can view the world's most uninteresting topics.

    Technologies used: JavaScript, React, Node.js, HTML, SCSS, CSS, MongoDB, Express, GraphQL, AWS S3, Heroku

    Github Live Site

    Log in or Sign up to Disinterest which utilizes encryption tools to keep your password safe.

    View the Disinterest index page that features all the user's pins and infinite scrolling.

    Take a look at any pin to see its comments and description!
